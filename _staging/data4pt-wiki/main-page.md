## Discover, Verify, Connect

  
\***Want to become aware of NAP and related standards?** [Explore the DATA4PT project pages](https://data4pt-project.eu/) and enjoy a wealth of knowledge\!

  - **Need to verify your NeTEx data?**

A NeTEx validation tool is planned to be delivered in the frame of the NAPCORE project. Meanwhile, [contact us here](mailto:technical.support@itxpt.org?subject=NeTEx%20validation%20tool%20\(from%20DATA4PT%20wiki\)) for any questions or requests about this topic.

  - **Require support?**

Don't hesitate\! [Fill in a support request](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1xQD5pPrxOFvoQrheQ0jpnFtWR3oWdCy5DyUZBRvWr0c/viewform?edit_requested=true), and we're here to help.

  - **Like to comment on the available pages in the "Discussion" tab?**

We value your input\! [Contact us](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1xQD5pPrxOFvoQrheQ0jpnFtWR3oWdCy5DyUZBRvWr0c/viewform?edit_requested=true) and share your thoughts.

  - **Wish to contribute and create new pages?**

[Join our expert community](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1xQD5pPrxOFvoQrheQ0jpnFtWR3oWdCy5DyUZBRvWr0c/viewform?edit_requested=true) and be part of something great\!

## Why DATA4PT?

DATA4PT is your one-stop destination for public transportation data, standards, and collaboration. Whether you're a newcomer or an expert, we're here to support your journey.

Join us in shaping the future of public transportation data together\! </big>
